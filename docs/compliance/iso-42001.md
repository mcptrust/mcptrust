# ISO/IEC 42001 – AI Management System

> [!IMPORTANT]
> MCPTrust provides **evidence and supports controls**—it is **not a certification** or formal compliance attestation.

## Overview

[ISO/IEC 42001](https://www.iso.org/standard/81230.html) specifies requirements for AI management systems. MCPTrust helps collect evidence for supply chain and risk management controls.

## Control Mapping

| Control Area | What MCPTrust Helps With | Evidence | How to Validate |
|--------------|--------------------------|----------|-----------------|
| **8.2** – AI system lifecycle | Artifact pinning and provenance verification | `mcp-lock.json`, SLSA attestations | `mcptrust artifact verify --deep mcp-lock.json` |
| **9.2** – Risk assessment | Automated tool risk classification | Scan output with risk levels | `mcptrust scan -- <server command>` |
| **9.4** – Supply chain mgmt | Integrity verification, provenance tracking | Lockfile with SRI hashes | `mcptrust verify --lockfile mcp-lock.json` |

## Generating Evidence

```bash
# Generate policy explain report
mcptrust policy explain --preset strict --json > iso-evidence.json

# Deep artifact verification
mcptrust artifact verify --deep mcp-lock.json

# Policy check with strict preset
mcptrust policy check --preset strict --lockfile mcp-lock.json -- "<server command>"
```
